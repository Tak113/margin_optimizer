Find an Optimum Gross Margin
If we continue storing win-loss dataset from quotes and map out win and loss count as a function of gross margin, it is expected that those empirical data for win and loss quotes will create a 2 different sets of distribution. From distributions, we could then generate probability of Win at each gross margin. Given a total life time volume and average ASP from each packaging type, expected revenue at each gross margin is computed as;
f_Profit(GM) = ASP * Volume * GM * Win_Prob(GM) - Cost of Sales * Total N of Quotes * Loss_Prob(GM)
